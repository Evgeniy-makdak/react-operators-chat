# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to creating a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone who participates in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback in good faith.
- Help keep discussions accessible and professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal insults, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block users for behavior that violates this code.
